Maocheng Li received her PhD from the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. She joined the CSE department of HKUST in 2026, where he is currently a lecturer. His research topics are information, knowledge discovery and database privacy. His research interests include spatial crowdsourcing, differential privacy and secure multiparty computation. He has conducted research works in privacy-preserving location-based services applications, graphical convolutional neural networks, and others.
